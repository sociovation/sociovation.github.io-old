Sociovation operates in the spaces between sectors and among a vibrant mix of people, projects and organizations. We’re creating new spaces to catalyze and support social innovation. Child Education And Development

"A child without education is like a bird without wings" ~ Believing in the same ideology, the centre for social innovation chooses " Child Education and development " as one of the key areas to work upon. Education is the basic human right and a significant factor in the development of children. It creates awareness and expands one's vision. Moreover, opening classroom doors to all the children will also break the intergenerational chains of poverty because education is intrinsically linked to all the development goals. Children of the underprivileged sections of the society have a desire to study, but unfortunately they are deprived of the resources and don't have access to any such means. So, efforts are being made by the team members to promote education for these underprivileged children. Workshops regarding the importance of education  are organised, teaching sessions are arranged for the children of nearby slums. Regular classes for these children will also be held in the college campus itself. The unparalleled combination of bounteous energy and unrelenting dedication shown by the members is sure to bring  a change in the society."


Women Empowerment

We are driven by the vision of a world where all women – in particular women from underprivileged backgrounds – enjoy full citizenship, earn a livelihood with dignity and generate wealth and value for all.

Our mission is to equip resource-poor women with knowledge and skills so that they excel as professionals and entrepreneurs, and earn a “livelihood with dignity” in jobs and markets that had traditionally been closed to them. We believe that when women are socially and economically empowered, they become catalysts of change – not just in their own lives, but also in their families and communities. We work with resource-poor women to enable them to:

· imagine new roles for themselves · get opportunities to work in safe and respectful environments.
· enhance not just their economic status but also their self-respect and dignity
· build social capital as they transform their lives and lives of people around them.
